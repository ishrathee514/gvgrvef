About us

ISTE-VIT presents to you Horizon'21 - a national technical fest with over 1400 participants for a learning experience like none other. Expand your skill set with workshops on Web development, Photo and Video design, Python Automation and Competitive Coding. Initiate new ideas , put forth innovative solutions and compete with sharpest of minds in one a kind hackathon - Technica.

Web Dev

Web Dev is a hands-on workshop in Horizon, where you learn all about building websites and managing databases. Equip yourself with cutting edge web development tools, all through real-time teaching and interesting exercises. Take home a unique web development project, all done by you!

Date : 9th April 2021

Register

Automate.py

Automate.py is a hands-on workshop where you will learn the ins and outs of virtual assistants. Learn how to automate systems with a series of projects, make your own Alexa or Siri with basic Python programming, all from scratch!

Date: 9th April 2021

Register

Photonix

Photonix is a hands on workshop, made for the artist in you. Traverse the world of poster and UI design and learn to create the most stunning visuals and imagery using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. A workshop to explore your creativity and convert ideas and design to life!

Date : 6th & 7th April 2021

Register

Code Asymptote

Code asymptote is a hands on workshop, where you step into the world of data structures. Learn all about queues, linked list and discover various DSA algorithms. Understand how they are implemented, know how to apply them. Learn, understand, implement, all from scratch!

Date : 20th March 2021

Register

Technica

Initiate | Innovate | Inspire

Technica is a 48-hour Hack-a-Thon conducted by ISTE-VIT. The hack is aimed at cultivating a technical temper among the student community and promoting healthy competition among peers. It is a one of a kind opportunity for participants to develop their technical skills and develop innovative and exciting solutions to real world problems!
